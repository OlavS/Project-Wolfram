How it works

Author/date/time:

For the nurse and health care worker:





1. The user logs on at the start of his or her shift (only needs to logon one time).











2. The assignment list is displayed with GPS coordinates for navigation.











3. When the user is at the patients location he or she scans the QR code on the inside of the patients door to log arrival time and get the patients info, work assignment and messages from, doctor, family, ealthcareworker or other nurse.





4. After completing the task the user checks the boxes and adds comment witch are viewable only to the persons checked in the list. So that only the people that need to see the comments get them. The comment section also supports pictures so it is easy to track wounds or infection. The user only needs to write the comment one time and share it with the relevant people. It is also possible to order food from kolonial.no that delivers food home to the patient. The family can also order and see what has been ordered and delivered.



For the family:





1. The family member given access logs on in the app.











2. The time table is displayed and the family member can see who has been there. Comments will show messages shared with the family and what has been done. It is also possible to order food from kolonial.no that delivers food home to the patient. The family can see what has been ordered and delivered.








3. The family can also make comments and to send to:
• Health care workers
• Nurse
• Driver
• Senior center
• State
If the patient is on vacation it is easy to inform that relevant parties that they don’t need to come and if the assignments are not done it is easy to send a message to the state. Also the family can let the health care workers now if the patient needs to wear any special clothes or other needs.



For the doctor:





1. The Doctor logs on in the app











2. The doctor can access the patient’s history, comments, medication, pictures and see that the patient is getting the right care.











3. If needed the Doctor can send messages to the relevant parties







For the driver:





1. The driver logs on at the start of his or her shift (only needs to logon one time).











2. The assignment list is displayed with GPS coordinates for navigation.











3. When the driver picks up or delivers a patient he or she logs the event and it is displayed in the patient history. The family can see the information in the timetable.

4. The driver can send messages to the state, family or senior center if a patient has any special needs.





For the senior center:


Health care worker at the senior center can log inn and see patient info, relevant comments and send messages to Health care worker, Doctor, Family, Nurse, State, and Driver If needed.





For the state:


The State employee given access can log inn and see:
• That the work assignments given by the state are done.
• Read and send messages to the relevant parties.
• In case of complaints from family the state can view the patient’s history and resolve the problems.
• Read recommendations from
o Health care worker
o Doctor
o Family
o Nurse
o State
o Senior center
o Driver
