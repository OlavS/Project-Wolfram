The issue

Written by Huy Minh Le, 03.11.2016 20:36:
Our concept is based on research done in the norwegian health care system. A demand for a better infrastructure considering patients, health personnell, and family members.

With the ever growing life expectancy, it is inevitable that there will be more elderly people, and as a consequence, the use of health care workers are more and more common. The problem lies with the unprofessional and unorganized workers. There has been too many instances where the worker either forgets to tend to one of the patients, or forgets to give the patients their necessities, for example his/her medicine or supplements. In addition, the patients are commonly affected by dementia, and they may not even remember if they have eaten, or if they have been taken care of. Because of this, the faith of the families using this service for their elderly loved ones will diminish, and the use of the service may be an unpleasant experience.

Advantages:

  • More efficient
  • Grants better communication
  • Lower chance of wrong treatment
  • Increased security for the patient
  • Lowers logistics costs
  • Easy to use
  • A complete solution
